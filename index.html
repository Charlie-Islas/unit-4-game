Rules of da house! (Read carefully)

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are foour crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose if your total score goes above the random number at any point.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click it. Each time the game starts, the value of each crystal will change.
